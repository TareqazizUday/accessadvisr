About AccessAdvisr

AccessAdvisr exists to give disabled people a stronger voice in shaping accessibility. We provide a platform, supported by our website and social media channels, where people can rate, review, and discuss the accessibility—or inaccessibility—of different places. By sharing real experiences, we aim to make accessibility information transparent, practical, and genuinely useful for everyone.

Core Values Driving AccessAdvisr

Since its inception, AccessAdvisr has grown into a trusted accessibility platform, empowering people with disabilities worldwide. We actively engage with our community through YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, BlueSky, and LinkedIn, sharing insights, updates, and initiatives that reflect our commitment to inclusion.
